About Us

We plan and source healthy and tasty meals so you don’t have to. Our meal kits deliver you weekly pre-prepped mise en place (aka, everything in its place) for you to enjoy a warm home-cooked gourmet meal without the hassles of shopping and prepping. Every week’s menu’s a surprise! Sign up now to get your first kit free!

Sustainable

Convenience often comes at the price of the environment. Not at Gourmet Kits! We only use stainless steel, glass, cloth and paper packaging, and offer packaging returns for returning customers to help you save more! We also source our produce from local farmers, helping not just the local community thrive but also keep our carbon footprint down.
